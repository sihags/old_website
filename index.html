I am a third year Ph.D student in the ECSE department at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. I am also broadly interested in the areas of graphical signal processing and optimization.

In Fall 2018, I worked as a research intern with Computational Neuroscience and Multiscale Brain Modeling group at IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Center.
